#define EU868_MAX_NB_BANDS   6

Maximum number of bands

We have broken down EU-868 MHz BAND 2 into two parts. That's why total number of sub-bands is 6. from 863 MHz to 865 MHz region is part of BAND 2, however we call it Band-5 here. Duty cycle limit is 0.1 % in this sub band.
